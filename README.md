🛒 Retail Customer Analytics & Market Basket Analysis
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail transaction data to extract actionable business insights that can improve customer retention, sales performance, and cross-selling strategies. The analysis emphasizes:

Customer segmentation (RFM)
Sales trends & seasonality
Product associations (Market Basket Analysis)
🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses often face challenges such as:

Identifying high-value customers
Understanding purchase behavior
Increasing average order value
Reducing customer churn

This project uses data-driven techniques to address these issues effectively.

📊 Key Insights
📌 1. Market Basket Analysis (Association Rules)
Findings: Strong pairing of products with different colors (e.g., Red ↔ Green Alarm Clocks, Pink ↔ Red).
Insights: Customers tend to buy products with variations (e.g., Teacups & Saucers, Lunch Boxes).
Strategy:
Create combo bundles (e.g., “Buy 2 colors, get a discount”)
Show "Frequently Bought Together" recommendations
Place similar variants side-by-side (online & in-store)
📌 2. Top 10 Customers by Revenue
Findings: A few customers contribute significantly to the revenue (~280K).
Insights:
The business heavily depends on top customers (VIP segment).
Revenue distribution follows the Pareto principle: 20% of customers generate 80% of revenue.
Strategy:
Create VIP loyalty programs offering early access, exclusive discounts, and personalized recommendations.
Focus on retaining top customers rather than just acquiring new ones.
📌 3. Top 10 Products
Findings: Identified the top 10 most popular products.
Insights: High-value products driving sales.
Strategy:
Bundle frequently purchased products.
Promote high-margin items to boost profitability.
📌 4. Sales Seasonality & Trends
Findings: Sales performance is impacted by seasonality, with spikes during holidays and specific months.
Insights:
Optimizing promotions during peak seasons can maximize sales.
Understanding seasonal trends can help plan inventory and marketing strategies.
Strategy:
Focus on increased marketing and product visibility during peak seasons.
Optimize inventory and pricing based on sales trends.
📌 5. Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)
Findings: Customers are segmented based on their recency, frequency, and monetary values.
Insights:
High-value, repeat customers are critical to the business.
Understanding segmentation can help personalize marketing and retention strategies.
Strategy:
Tailor marketing efforts based on customer segments (e.g., exclusive offers for high-value customers).
Develop retention programs for less frequent customers.
📌 6. Country by Revenue
Findings: The distribution of revenue across different countries.
Insights:
Certain countries contribute significantly more revenue than others.
Analyzing revenue by country can help in targeting specific regions for marketing efforts.
Strategy:
Focus on high-revenue countries for targeted marketing.
Identify countries with growth potential and tailor promotions accordingly.
📌 7. Monthly Revenue
Findings: Analysis of revenue trends over time, broken down by month.
Insights:
Revenue varies seasonally, with peak months during holidays and special events.
Understanding monthly trends can help plan promotions and inventory.
Strategy:
Increase marketing efforts during peak months.
Adjust inventory and pricing strategies based on monthly revenue patterns.
